A plugin to hide post titles, dates, and author names on WordPress posts and pages.

== Description ==

ContentRemover allows you to selectively hide (remover) the title, date, and author information on individual posts or pages through a metabox option. Ideal for sites that need to display content without standard metadata.

**Plugin Features:**
- Hide post and page titles
- Remove publication dates
- Hide author (bio) information
- Customize display settings for specific post types
- Compatible with most WordPress themes

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `contentremover` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Edit a post or page, and use the "Content Remover Settings" metabox to hide specific metadata.
